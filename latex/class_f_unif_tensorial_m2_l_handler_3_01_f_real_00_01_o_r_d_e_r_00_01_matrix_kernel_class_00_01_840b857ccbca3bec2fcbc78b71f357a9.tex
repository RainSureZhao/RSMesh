The M2L operation $X+=C_tY$ is performed in Fourier space by application of the convolution theorem (i.\+e. $FX+=FC_t:FY$), where $FY$ is the transformed multipole expansion and $FX$ is the transformed local expansion, both padded with zeros and of size $r_c=(2\times ORDER-1)^3$ or $r_c^{opt}=\frac{r_c}{2}+1$ for real valued kernels. The index $t$ denotes the transfer vector of the target cell to the source cell.
